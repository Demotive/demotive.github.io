This month, we are very excited to be publishing the latest nature writing from eminent ornithologist and wildlife journalist Dominic Couzens, as he takes us on a personal journey to track down the science behind bird behaviour.

From dawn ‘til dusk, birds do things that are surprising and mystifying. Songs of Love and War delves into the surprisingly dark secret lives of birds.

Including interviews with experts and naturalists, the book is more than just an inside look at bird behaviour - it also represents a personal journey of discovery. What starts as a desire to learn more about the birds encountered on a regular father-and-son walk through the woods leads to a realisation that a bird's life is very far from the idyllic scene that can often be glimpsed by the casual birdwatcher.

This is Dominic Couzens at his best. Click below to read an extract from Songs of Love and War over on the Bloomsbury Wildlife blog.
